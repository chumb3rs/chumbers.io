Marking some of the best months of my young life, I was accepted in late 2022 to complete an exchange semester at Purdue University for Spring 2023. Those days in West Lafayette remain some of the most formative of my life.

Here, I try to pay homage to some of those moments: the sites, the sounds, and most importantly, the people I met along the journey.
